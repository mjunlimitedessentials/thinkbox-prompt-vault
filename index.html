Sales

RCTF PROMPT ✦

Cold DM / LinkedIn Outreach
Land in inboxes and get responses with this power outreach script.
RCTF PROMPT ✦

Write a short, warm DM (under 100 words) that references something genuine about them, creates curiosity, and ends with a soft CTA. Do not start with "Hey" or use emojis.
