What is Lovable?

Lovable is an AI-powered website builder that creates fully functional, professional websites just from your plain-English descriptions. No HTML, CSS, JavaScript, or any technical knowledge required.

Think of it like having a professional web developer on call 24/7 — you describe what you want, and Lovable builds it in real time right in front of you.

⭐

Who is this for? Business owners, coaches, creators, ministry leaders, marketers — anyone who wants a professional website without paying a developer thousands of dollars or spending months learning to code.

Step 3

Start Your First Project with AI Prompts

This is where the magic happens. You describe your website in plain English and Lovable builds it. The key is writing a clear, specific prompt.

What to Include in Your Starting Prompt

  • What type of site — landing page, portfolio, business website, online store, church site
  • Your business name & what you do
  • The look & feel — modern, elegant, bold, minimalist, dark, colorful
  • Key sections needed — hero, about, services, pricing, testimonials, contact form
  • Colors or style references — "like Apple's website" or "purple and gold"

Example Prompts — Copy & Adapt These

🏪 Local Business Example
Create a professional website for a cleaning company called "Sparkle Pro Cleaning." Use a clean white and sky-blue color scheme. Include: a hero section with a bold headline and a "Get a Free Quote" button, a Services section showing residential, commercial, and deep cleaning, a testimonials section with 3 customer reviews, a simple pricing table with 3 tiers, and a contact form. The tone should be trustworthy, friendly, and professional.
🎨 Creative Portfolio Example
Build a sleek, dark portfolio website for a freelance graphic designer. Use black, white, and gold accents throughout. Include: a bold hero with the name "Alex Carter — Visual Designer", a portfolio grid showing 6 sample project thumbnails, an About Me section, a Skills list, and a contact form with social links. Make it look editorial and high-end.
⛪ Ministry / Church Example
Create a welcoming website for "New Life Community Church." Use warm, calming colors — cream, soft gold, and deep navy. Include: a hero with a welcome message and service times, an About Our Church section, a Sermons/Messages section, an Upcoming Events section, a Giving/Donate section, and a Contact page. The tone should be warm, faith-based, and inviting to first-time visitors.
🛒 Online Store / Coaching Example
Build a landing page for my online coaching business called "Elevate Your Life Coaching." Use a bold purple and white color scheme. Include: a hero section with my tagline "Transform Your Mindset, Transform Your Life", a benefits section listing 4 key outcomes clients get, a Meet the Coach section with a photo placeholder and bio, a 3-tier pricing/packages section, and a "Book a Free Call" button throughout. Make it feel premium, motivational, and conversion-focused.

Step 4

Refine Your Design Visually

Don't like a font? Want to change a color? You have two easy ways to make changes — no code needed.

Option A — Chat with the AI

Simply type your change request in the chat panel on the left, just like texting a designer. Examples:

💬 Refinement Prompt Examples
"Change the background of the hero to dark navy blue." "Make all the buttons rounded with a gradient from purple to pink." "Replace the placeholder logo text with a flame emoji and 'BrandName'." "Add a sticky navigation bar at the top with links: Home, Services, About, Contact." "Move the testimonials section above the pricing section." "Make the fonts bigger and easier to read on mobile."

Option B — Visual Edits Mode (Point & Click)

For quick styling changes, use the built-in visual editor — completely free to use within daily limits.

  1. In the prompt box at the bottom-left, click the "Visual edits" button (the cursor icon).
  2. Your live preview becomes clickable — hover over any element and it highlights in purple.
  3. Click any element (text, button, image, section) to select it.
  4. A sidebar panel appears where you can change colors, fonts, padding, size, and text directly.
  5. Hold Ctrl (Windows) or Cmd (Mac) to select multiple elements at once.
  6. Click Send when satisfied — your changes apply instantly.
💡

Visual edits are free! You get 100 free visual edits per day. Use these for small styling tweaks and save your AI credits for bigger structural changes.

Undoing Mistakes — The Revert Button

Made a change you don't like? Every single AI response has a Revert option. Click the three-dot menu (⋯) next to any chat message and select "Revert" to go back to that version instantly.

⚠️

Always test on mobile view before publishing! Click the mobile icon in the top toolbar to preview how your site looks on a phone screen.

Step 5

Add Pages, Content & Images

Adding New Pages

To add a new page (like an About page or Blog), just ask Lovable in the chat:

💬 Adding Pages
"Add an About page with sections for our story, our team, and our values." "Create a Contact page with a contact form, phone number, address, and map embed." "Add a Blog page with a grid of 3 sample blog post cards." "Create a Services page that lists all 5 of our services in detail with pricing."

Replacing Placeholder Images

Lovable fills your site with placeholder images. To replace them:

  1. Use Visual Edits mode — click any image in the preview and upload your own from your computer.
  2. Or paste an image URL from a free stock photo site like Unsplash.com or Pexels.com into the image field.
  3. For AI-generated images, click any image in visual edits mode and choose "Generate with AI" — describe the image you want.

Adding Real Business Info

Once the structure looks good, update all placeholder text with your real content:

  • Real business name, tagline, and description
  • Your actual phone number, email, and address
  • Real service names, descriptions, and prices
  • Your own photos or brand images
  • Social media links (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.)
💡

You can paste all your content into one big prompt: "Update the site with my real business info: Name is [X], phone is [Y], services are [Z]…" Lovable will fill it all in for you.

Step 6

Publish Your Website Live

When your site looks great, it's time to go live. Publishing in Lovable takes about 30 seconds.

  1. In the editor, click the purple "Publish" button in the top-right corner.
  2. The Publish modal opens. Review your website URL — by default it will be something like your-project-name.lovable.app. You can customize this slug.
  3. Configure website access — on free/pro plans this is public (anyone with the link can see it). Business plans can restrict to workspace members only.
  4. Add your site title and description (these show up in Google search results — very important for SEO!).
  5. Upload a favicon (the small icon in browser tabs) — ideally your logo.
  6. Click "Run Security Check" — Lovable will scan for common security issues before you go live.
  7. Click the big "Publish" button. In 30–60 seconds your site is live!
  8. You'll get a pop-up with your live link. Share it, test it, celebrate! 🎉

Step 7

Connect a Custom Domain

A custom domain turns yoursite.lovable.app into www.yourbusiness.com — making your site look professional and trustworthy. Custom domains require a paid plan.

⭐

A custom domain is one of the best investments for your online presence. It builds brand credibility, improves Google search rankings, and makes your site memorable.

Option A — Buy a Domain Through Lovable (Easiest)

Lovable can register a domain for you and configure everything automatically — no technical setup required.

1

Open Domain Settings

In your project, go to Settings → Domains, or click the Publish modal → Custom domain link.

2

Click "Buy New Domain"

Search for your desired domain name. You'll see pricing (typically $10–$20/year for .com). Premium domains cost more.

3

Review Your Order

Choose your registration term (1–10 years). Enable auto-renew to prevent your domain from expiring accidentally.

4

Enter Registration Details

Fill in your contact information as required by ICANN (the global domain authority). Your privacy is protected automatically by WHOIS privacy.

5

Complete Payment via Stripe

Lovable processes payment securely through Stripe. Once confirmed, your domain is registered and DNS is configured automatically.

6

Wait for "Live" Status

Setup completes in a few minutes (up to a few hours). When your domain shows Live, your site is accessible at your new domain. 🎉

Option B — Connect a Domain You Already Own

If you already have a domain from GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google Domains, or elsewhere, you can connect it manually.

  1. In Settings → Domains, click "Connect Domain".
  2. Type in your existing domain name (e.g., mybusiness.com).
  3. Lovable will detect your domain provider and may be able to connect automatically via Entri. Click Authorize if prompted.
  4. If automatic setup does not work, choose Manual Setup — Lovable gives you two DNS records to add: an A record and a TXT record.
  5. Log into your domain provider (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.), go to DNS Settings, and add both records exactly as shown.
  6. Click Done in Lovable. DNS changes can take up to 72 hours to propagate worldwide, but usually complete within a few hours.
  7. When the domain shows Live status, you're done!

Step 8

Optimize for SEO (Get Found on Google)

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is what helps your website show up when people search Google for your type of business. Lovable has built-in tools to help.

Basic SEO Setup in Lovable

  1. Set a great Page Title — In the Publish modal, write a clear title like "Sparkle Pro Cleaning | Chicago Residential Cleaning Service". Include your city and main service for local SEO.
  2. Write a meta description — A 1–2 sentence summary of your page that appears in Google search results. Make it compelling and include a keyword: "Professional residential and commercial cleaning in Chicago. Book online and get 10% off your first clean."
  3. Upload a Social Share Image — When someone shares your link on social media, this image appears. Use your logo or a branded graphic (1200×630px is the ideal size).
  4. Run the SEO Review — In the Publish modal, click "Run SEO Review". Lovable checks your site for common SEO issues and gives you suggestions to fix them.
  5. Connect Google Search Console (after publishing with a custom domain) — Lovable guides you through verifying your site with Google so it gets indexed faster.

Ask Lovable to Improve Your SEO

💬 SEO Improvement Prompts
"Add proper heading structure (H1, H2, H3) to improve SEO throughout the site." "Add alt text descriptions to all images on the site." "Create a dedicated page for each of my main services to improve search rankings." "Add a FAQ section about our cleaning services — use common search questions as the questions." "Make the page load faster by optimizing the layout."
💡

Local SEO tip: Include your city name naturally throughout your site — in the hero headline, in service descriptions, and in the footer. This helps you rank for local searches like "plumber in Dallas" or "bakery near me."

Step 10

Final Launch Checklist ✅

Before you share your website with the world, run through this checklist to make sure everything is polished and professional.

Content & Design

  • All placeholder text has been replaced with real business information
  • Your real phone number, email, and address are displayed correctly
  • All placeholder images have been replaced with real photos or branded visuals
  • Your logo (or business name) appears clearly in the header/navigation
  • Every page has a clear headline that tells visitors what you do
  • There is at least one clear call-to-action button (Book Now, Contact Us, Get a Quote, etc.)
  • All links work — navigation, buttons, and social media links
  • The contact form works and sends to the correct email

Mobile & Performance

  • Site looks great on mobile (click the phone icon in the Lovable toolbar to preview)
  • Text is readable on small screens — no tiny fonts or overflowing content
  • Images load quickly and are not blurry
  • Navigation works properly on mobile (hamburger menu if applicable)

SEO & Metadata

  • Page title is set with your business name and main service/location
  • Meta description is written (1–2 compelling sentences with a keyword)
  • Favicon (tab icon) is set — ideally your logo
  • Social share image is uploaded (shows when link is shared on social media)
  • Lovable SEO Review has been run and major issues are resolved

Domain & Publishing

  • Site is published and the live URL works correctly
  • Custom domain is connected and shows "Live" status (paid plans)
  • Both www and non-www versions of your domain redirect correctly
  • SSL certificate is active (you should see the padlock icon in the browser — Lovable handles this automatically)

After Launch

  • Share your new website on social media
  • Add your website URL to your business cards, email signature, and all social profiles
  • Submit your site to Google Search Console (Lovable guides you through this)
  • Create a Google Business Profile listing if you serve local customers
  • Set a monthly reminder to review and update your site content
⭐

Congratulations! You now have a fully professional website live on the internet. Remember — your website is never truly "done." Keep it updated with fresh content, new offers, and current information to get the most out of it.
