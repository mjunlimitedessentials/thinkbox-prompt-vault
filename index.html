Business Growth Prompts
Sales, outreach, offers & client acquisition
Sales
High-Converting Sales Email
Use this to write a sales email for any offer in under 60 seconds.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are an expert copywriter who specializes in email marketing for service-based businesses. I run [BUSINESS NAME] and I offer [SERVICE/PRODUCT]. My ideal client is [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Write a compelling sales email with a subject line, opening hook, body that addresses their pain point, and a clear call-to-action to [DESIRED ACTION]. Format it as a ready-to-send email, conversational and no longer than 200 words.
Outreach
Cold DM / LinkedIn Outreach
Land in inboxes and get responses with this power outreach script.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a business development expert who writes conversational, non-salesy outreach messages. I am [YOUR NAME], founder of [BUSINESS]. I want to reach out to [TARGET PERSON/ROLE] on [PLATFORM]. My goal is [OUTCOME — e.g. book a discovery call, collaborate, get a referral]. Write a short, warm DM (under 100 words) that references something genuine about them, creates curiosity, and ends with a soft CTA. Do not start with "Hey" or use emojis.
Offer
Irresistible Offer Builder
Build a $1K–$10K offer that practically sells itself.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a premium offer strategist who helps service providers package their expertise into high-ticket offers. My business is [BUSINESS NAME]. I help [AUDIENCE] achieve [RESULT] through [METHOD]. My current price range is [RANGE]. Build me a complete offer breakdown including: a compelling name, what's included, bonuses, price anchor, and a one-sentence "what you get" statement. Format as a clean offer sheet.
Strategy
90-Day Business Growth Plan
Get a full strategic roadmap customized to your business in minutes.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a business growth strategist with expertise in online service businesses. My business is [NAME], I offer [SERVICES], my audience is [AUDIENCE], and my current monthly revenue is [RANGE]. Create a 90-day growth plan broken into 3 phases: (1) Foundation, (2) Momentum, (3) Scale. Each phase should include 3 specific action steps, one revenue-generating activity, and one metric to track. Format as a table.

Content Creation Prompts
Social media, captions, hooks & video scripts
Instagram
7-Day Content Calendar
Never run out of content ideas — get a full week in one prompt.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a social media strategist who specializes in content for personal brands and service businesses. My brand is [NAME], I am a [TITLE], and I serve [AUDIENCE]. My content pillars are [PILLAR 1], [PILLAR 2], [PILLAR 3]. Create a 7-day Instagram content calendar with: post type (Reel, Carousel, Static), topic, hook, caption (under 150 words), and 5 hashtags per day. Format as a table.
Hooks
Scroll-Stopping Hook Generator
10 viral hooks for any topic that stop the scroll instantly.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a viral content strategist who writes hooks that stop the scroll. I am creating content about [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE] on [PLATFORM]. Generate 10 scroll-stopping hooks using these formats: 2 curiosity hooks, 2 bold statement hooks, 2 story hooks, 2 number/stat hooks, and 2 controversy/opinion hooks. Make each under 15 words. Label each by type.
Video
60-Second Reel Script
A complete reel script — hook, content, CTA — in under a minute.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a short-form video scriptwriter who creates viral, value-packed reels. I want to create a 60-second Instagram Reel about [TOPIC]. My audience is [AUDIENCE] and my goal is [GOAL — educate/sell/inspire/entertain]. Write a complete script with: a hook (first 3 seconds), 3 value points (10 seconds each), and a CTA (final 7 seconds). Include on-screen text suggestions in brackets.
Email
Email Newsletter Template
Write a binge-worthy newsletter your audience actually reads.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are an email newsletter strategist who writes conversational, high-open-rate content. I run [BUSINESS], my audience is [AUDIENCE], and this week's topic is [TOPIC]. Write a newsletter with: a subject line (max 8 words), preview text (max 10 words), personal opening (2 sentences), main value section (3 tips or insights), a soft plug for [OFFER], and a sign-off. Keep it under 400 words. Warm, direct, no fluff.

Ministry & Faith Prompts
Devotionals, sermons, outreach & community building
Devotional
Daily Devotional Writer
Create a powerful devotional for any scripture in seconds.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a Christian devotional writer with a warm, encouraging, and biblically-grounded voice. Using [SCRIPTURE REFERENCE AND VERSE], write a daily devotional for [AUDIENCE — e.g., entrepreneurs, women, ministers]. Include: a title, 2-paragraph reflection connecting the scripture to everyday life, a practical application step, and a closing prayer (under 50 words). Format for a social media post or email.
Outreach
Ministry Event Promotion Copy
Fill your events with compelling faith-based promotional content.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a Christian marketing specialist who writes compelling promotional content for ministry events. My ministry is [MINISTRY NAME], and we are hosting [EVENT NAME] on [DATE] at [LOCATION/ONLINE]. The theme is [THEME] and it is for [AUDIENCE]. Write: a Facebook post, an Instagram caption with hashtags, a WhatsApp broadcast message, and a short email invite. Keep the tone warm, Spirit-led, and exciting.

AI Automation Playbooks
Systems, workflows & automation blueprints
Automation
Client Onboarding System
Build a complete automated client onboarding flow from scratch.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are an operations systems expert who designs automated client onboarding workflows for service businesses. My business is [NAME], I offer [SERVICE], and my clients are [AUDIENCE]. Map out a complete automated onboarding system including: welcome email sequence (3 emails), intake form questions (10), first call agenda, client portal checklist, and 30-day check-in touchpoint. Format each section clearly with actionable steps.
Workflow
Content Repurposing Machine
Turn one piece of content into 10+ assets automatically.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a content strategist who specializes in repurposing content across multiple platforms. I have this [BLOG POST / VIDEO TRANSCRIPT / PODCAST EPISODE]: [PASTE CONTENT]. Repurpose it into: 3 Instagram captions, 1 LinkedIn article intro (150 words), 5 Twitter/X threads, 1 email newsletter section, 3 short-form Reel hooks, and 1 Pinterest pin description. Maintain my brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR VOICE].
AI Tools
AI Tool Stack Builder
Get a customized AI tool stack for your exact business type.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are an AI implementation consultant who helps small business owners build lean, effective AI tool stacks. My business type is [TYPE], my team size is [SIZE], my monthly budget for tools is [BUDGET], and my biggest time drains are [LIST 3]. Recommend a complete AI tool stack with: tool name, purpose, monthly cost, and how I use it daily. Prioritize free/low-cost options first. Format as a table.
SOP
AI-Powered SOP Creator
Document any business process as a repeatable SOP in minutes.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a business systems consultant who writes clear, actionable Standard Operating Procedures. I need an SOP for [PROCESS NAME] in my [BUSINESS TYPE]. This process involves [BRIEF DESCRIPTION]. The person following this SOP is [ROLE — e.g., VA, team member, or yourself]. Write a step-by-step SOP with: purpose statement, tools needed, step-by-step instructions (numbered), quality checkpoints, and a completion checklist. Keep language simple and clear.

Kids & Creative Education Prompts
Coloring books, activity books, reading lessons & interactive games
Coloring Book
Kids Coloring Book Creator
Generate a full themed coloring book concept with page descriptions — ready for illustration.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a children's book illustrator and creative director who designs educational coloring books for kids ages [AGE RANGE]. Create a complete coloring book concept on the theme of [THEME — e.g., animals, space, Bible stories, community helpers]. Include: a title, a 2-sentence description for the back cover, and 10 detailed page descriptions (what to draw on each page) with a simple one-line educational caption for each image. Format each page as: Page #, Scene Description, Educational Caption.
Activity Book
Kids Activity Book Builder
Build a complete activity book with puzzles, games, and learning exercises for any age group.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a childhood education specialist and curriculum designer who creates engaging activity books for kids ages [AGE RANGE]. Build a [NUMBER]-page activity book on the theme [THEME]. Include a mix of: word searches, fill-in-the-blank sentences, matching exercises, simple math puzzles, draw-and-color pages, and a short story with comprehension questions. Each activity should align with [GRADE LEVEL] learning standards. Format each activity with a title, instructions, and the activity content ready to layout.
Reading Lesson
Interactive Reading Lesson Plan
Create a fun, structured reading lesson for any age, skill level, or subject theme.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a literacy coach and elementary reading specialist. Create an interactive reading lesson for [GRADE/AGE LEVEL] students focused on [READING SKILL — e.g., phonics, sight words, comprehension, fluency]. The theme of the lesson is [THEME]. Include: a warm-up activity (5 min), a read-aloud passage (100–150 words, age-appropriate), 5 comprehension questions (2 literal, 2 inferential, 1 creative), a vocabulary section with 5 words and definitions, and a fun extension activity. Format as a teacher-ready lesson plan.
Story Book
Children's Story Book Writer
Write a complete illustrated children's story with a moral lesson built in.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are an award-winning children's book author who writes age-appropriate, culturally inclusive stories with positive messages. Write a complete children's picture book story for ages [AGE RANGE] about [MAIN CHARACTER — name, animal, or child] who learns the lesson of [MORAL/VALUE — e.g., kindness, perseverance, faith, sharing]. The setting is [SETTING]. Write 12–15 pages, 1–3 sentences per page, with an illustration description in brackets after each page of text. End with a discussion question for parents and kids.
Interactive Game
Educational Game Designer
Design a complete interactive learning game — digital or printable — for any subject.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are an educational game designer who creates engaging, curriculum-aligned learning games for kids ages [AGE RANGE]. Design a complete [GAME TYPE — e.g., board game, card game, quiz game, scavenger hunt] that teaches [SUBJECT/SKILL — e.g., multiplication, sight words, Bible verses, history facts]. Include: game objective, materials needed, setup instructions, step-by-step rules, 20 question/challenge cards with answers, and 3 variations for different skill levels (beginner, intermediate, advanced). Format as a complete printable game guide.
Faith-Based Kids
Bible Story Activity Pack
Turn any Bible story into a complete kids activity pack — perfect for Sunday school or homeschool.
RCTF PROMPT ✦
You are a Christian children's curriculum writer who creates faith-based activity packs for kids ages [AGE RANGE]. Using the Bible story of [STORY — e.g., David and Goliath, Noah's Ark, The Good Samaritan], create a complete activity pack including: a retold story (age-appropriate, 150 words), 3 discussion questions, a memory verse with a fun memorization tip, a coloring page description, a simple craft idea with instructions, and a take-home prayer the child can say. Format as a complete Sunday school or homeschool lesson.
